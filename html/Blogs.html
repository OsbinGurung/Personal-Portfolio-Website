How Internet technology has affected us in our daily lives.

Internet technology has changed how we live our lives now compared to the lifestyles of 20 years ago. We now are dependent upon internet technologies where most works are done with the help of internet technology. Internet technology has improved alot over the last decade and now Internet technologies like smartphones, laptops, computers, tablets,etc are available. Internet technology are now easy to get and is available to the general public. Internet technology is small and compact which makes it easier to travel with where you can fit a smartphone with capabilities of a computer inside your pockets. These changes has brought positives and negatives impact on our daily lives and society. Some of the changes brought by internet technology are written below.

Positive Impact

Internet technology has changed how we communicate


Internet technologies has changed our ways of communication alot. Internet technology like Email, smartphones, video chat, messaging apps, etc are some of the most used forms of internet communication used today. Since internet has been easily available to everyone, communication through the internet is possible. Messaging apps like Viber, Messenger, Whatsapp, Telegram has made communication much more easier as people can text each other through the internet via the messaging app. Mails are used for delivering large objects or items only and the use of mails for sending messages to others has been decreasing so much that people doesnot use mails to send messages alot. With the help of internet technology, a person is able to communicatio with another person living very far away with just a stable internet connection.Through the internet you can video call a person, send a text, email a person, watch online videos, conduct online classes, etc. This all is possible with the internet as it is more cost efficient that other means of communication. So in a sense, internet technology has redefined how we communication with eadch other.

Technology has changed how we pay bills and transfer money


Another aspect of our life that internet technology has changed is how we pay bills and transfer money. Before we used to pay our bills by going directly to the office and pay it and go to the bank to transfer money to someone else but now we can do it over the internet and has some benefits from it too. Using technologies like a smartphone, laptop or computer we can pay our bills and transfer money from our home with the help of an internet. This benefits us alot as we do not have to wait in line to pay our water bills or electricity bills as these could be paid online with the help of the internet from our home. The same also goes for transferring money as we can just do it online. Not only do we not have to stay in a line but we can also get confirmation that we have paid our bills or the other person has received the money we had just transferred and this is can be known after paying our bills or after sending money.

Technology has changed how we watch tv


The way we watch tv has also changed compared to how it was before. Before we used to watch television through a dedicated cable to watch but now with the internet we most probably do not require such cables dedicated for television only. Now with the advancement of the television, tv's now can connect to the internet and could be used to surf the internet too. With such feature, watching videos from Youtube is possible and even Netflix with the internet. Nowadays even tv remote even come with a Youtube and Netflix button. The way we used to watch tv has changed drastically to the point that some people only watch TV's for Youtube and Netflix only.

Technology has changed how we stay save


Before we used to keep security guards and dogs in our house to portect ourselves from intruders in our home. But with the advancement of Internet technology has made it possible to stay save inside and outside our homes. We have security camera, motion sensor cameras, gps in our phones, alarms to alert us and many more ways to keep ourself save. Even if we are outside, our phones and smart watches has a emergency function if we are in any danger, they could contact people in our emergency contact alerting them our location and even condtion. Some smart watches has feature that monitors our heartbeat and if our heatbeat is below the normal range and is decreasing then the smart watch alerts any emergency contact of ours. Internet technology has helped us to stay save anywhere we are.

Negative Impact

Technology has affected our sleeping habits


Many of us tend to stay up late at night texting friends or scrolling through social media. Since there are many entertainment contente online we tend to watch them once we get hooked to it. Many people tend to stay up late at night to watch a movie or series or even to play games online with their friends. Such habits has affected our sleeping habits. Lack of sleep or bad sleepipng habits can cause irritability, impulsive behaviour, anxiety, depression, weakens immunity system which harms both our physical and mental health. Technology has caused such problems to be seen in our society.

Cyber bullying and Cyber crimes has increased


The rise in Cyber bullying and Cyber crimes has increased in number since the increase in used of internet technology. Cyber bullying has increased alot as it is a form of bullying others online without having your identity known. This has caused an increase in suicide by mostly teenagers as a result of cyber bullying. Cyber crimes has also risen in number too. Cyber crimes like scams, fishing, hacking, identity theft, stealing personal or important information, online fraud and many more has increased. Due to this many people are a victim to this unkowningly. Most people lose personal infromation, money and even devices are attacked making them inoperatable. Internet technology has given a new form of bullying and crimes.

Explicit content are more easily available to the children


Many parents are concerned for their children as explicit contents and violence are easily accessible on the internet. Even when using parental controls on the devices that a children use, they are exposed to explicit and violent content in one point or the other. This can cause the childrens thinking capabilities to become more negative and cause change in behaviour causing aggression and lack of empathy. Such things happen as they are exposed to the explicit and violent content online making them lack of enpathy towards others.

Children's are addicted to playing online games


Online games are easily accessible and many children get addicted to them. Mostly due to their friends influence a child starts playing games but if the child starts to get addicted towards them it will cause some problems for the child. The child would not fully develop social skills, get aggressive towards others and will only want to play games instead of doing their works. This has caused alot of changes in a child personality in which they become addicted to the internet and does not do any work or go outside to play. The children gets less exercise and does not have good social skills. Internet has caused childrens to be addicted to games and their devices.

Overall Verdict


In conclusion, internet and it's technology has brought alot of changes in our daily lives. Some good and some bad but overall we can say that internet technology has brought alot of positive changes around and has in some ways helped us in our lives. The positive effect of internet technologies outweighs the negative impacts but the negative impacts are still available. Some rules or restriction can be used to minimise the negative impacts of internet on our daily lives. We can only minimise the negative effects of internet technology by being aware, limiting the use of internet and to use the internet safely and securely.
